About Shradha

This is a civil society organisation for differently abled was formed on December 1999 with a dedicated and committed group of volunteers,social activitities, Doctors, Professionals and with the help and cooperation of other civil society organizations rendering services as welfare and developments. The focus activity of the society is to provide institutional and community based rehabilitation to enhance the quality of life of the person with disabilities and their families, meet their basic needs, and ensure their inclusion and participation.

Our Vision

People with disabilites are EQUAL PARTNERS IN ALL RESPECTS AND ALL FIELDS.

Our Mission

To strengthen the disability movement through research, training , advocacy and public education for full and equal participation of people with disabilites in the country's mainstream.

We Believes

In the right to equality of opportunity.

In the right to living with dignity.

In the right to information.

That disability is a human right issue.
